# TiltCheck
Welcome to TiltCheck — the ecosystem built *for degens, by a degen*, delivering fairness, safety, and transparency without killing the fun.

## Mission
Make gambling communities safer, smarter, and more transparent.

## Built For
Degens, creators, mods, and server owners who want tools that actually work.

## What It Does
- Tracks casino behavior
- Protects users from scams
- Detects tilt patterns
- Encourages smart play
- Powers community tools

## Why It Exists
Because the gambling world is messy, casinos hide the ball, and users deserve receipts, consistency, and real support.

## Trust Engines

### Casino Trust Engine
Real-time scoring for casinos based on real data — no bullshit.

- Fairness consistency (AI checks)
- Payout behavior
- Bonus stability
- User reports
- Link safety from FreeSpinScan
- Compliance events
- Support quality

Users get clear warnings, explanations, and alerts when casinos fuck up.

### Degen Trust Engine
Fair trust system for users — explainable and recoverable.

- Tilt indicators
- Community behavior
- Scam flags
- Accountability actions
- Tip/airdrop generosity

Levels: Very High to High-Risk. Always allows comeback, no shaming.

## Tilt Engine
Watches messages and patterns for tilt detection.

- Tilt scores
- Soft nudges
- Cooldown routing

## Tools

### JustTheTip
Non-custodial tipping, airdrops, rewards — no custody, flat fees.

- /tip and group sends
- TriviaDrops & AirDrops
- Flat-fee routing
- Swap fallback
- Anti-tilt safeguards

Fast, reliable, doesn't hold your money hostage.
