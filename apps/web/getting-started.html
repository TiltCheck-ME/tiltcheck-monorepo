Common Questions

Do I need to create an account?

No account creation required. Connect your Discord to access personalized features — everything else is available without login.

Is JustTheTip actually non-custodial?

Yes. TiltCheck never holds your funds. You sign transactions with your own wallet (Phantom, Solflare, x402). If TiltCheck disappeared tomorrow, your wallet is unaffected. See the full technical breakdown.

What does a trust score actually measure?

Casino trust scores track fairness, payout behavior, bonus nerfs, and compliance. Degen trust scores track community behavior, accountability tool usage, and scam flags — with automatic recovery. Read the full breakdown on Degen Trust and Trust Explained.

Can I use TiltCheck without a crypto wallet?

Yes. SusLink, trust scores, casino data, tilt tracking, and CollectClock work without any wallet. JustTheTip tipping requires a Solana wallet to send or receive SOL.
