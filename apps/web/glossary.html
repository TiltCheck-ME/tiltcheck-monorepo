TiltCheck Glossary

Quick definitions for common TiltCheck language so users, mods, and builders stay aligned.

Trust Score

Numeric confidence signal based on transparency, evidence, and community-confirmed behavior.

LinkCheck

URL scanner that flags scam domains, impersonation, and suspicious redirects before clicks happen.

BonusCheck

Bonus monitoring layer for nerf detection, term changes, and promo reliability tracking.

Gameplay Fairness Analyzer

Session-level fairness and behavior signals that surface anomalies and tilt-risk indicators.

VaultLock

Time-based wallet protection flow used to reduce impulsive withdrawals and lock profit windows.

Degen Trust

Community accountability signal built from verifiable behavior, not social clout.
