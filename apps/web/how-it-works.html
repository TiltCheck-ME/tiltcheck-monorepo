How TiltCheck Works

1. Discord Vibe Check

Sync the bot to your Discord so we can watch you lose your mind in real-time. No moralizing, we just read your server logs and intervene before you regret everything.

What we watch for:

  • Rapid message bursts (rage-typing)
  • Emotional language patterns (desperation, max copium)
  • Complaining about losses right before depositing again

2. The Reality Check Bot

We scan your chat and gameplay for signs of catastrophic tilt. Instead of a "Neural AI," we just look for obvious despair—like typing completely in all-caps or using classic "just one more spin" logic.

When tilt is detected, we calculate your Tilt Probability. High scores automatically trigger the Emergency Brake on your browser and ping your Discord squad so they can yell at you to stop.

3. Casino Trust Scores

Before you deposit blindly, we check the casino's trust score. If it's a scam, we tell you to your face:

  • Domain Floor — Brand new domains running fly-by-night operations.
  • Drainer Reports — Verified on-chain wallet drainers.
  • Withdrawal Friction — Casinos that let you deposit instantly but suddenly demand KYC when you try to withdraw $50.

4. The Emergency Brake (Phish Nuke)

We automatically swat your hand away from phishing links. If you try to click a link to a known drainer or a shadow-cloned casino, we freeze the page and yell at you. Stop blindly signing transactions.
