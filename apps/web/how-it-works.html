How TiltCheck Works

1. Discord Signal Sync

TiltCheck syncs with your Discord stack to monitor behavioral patterns in real-time. No moralizing, just cold data audit of your session history.

What we watch for:

  • Rapid message bursts (typing while tilted)
  • Emotional language patterns (frustration, anger)
  • Loss mentions followed by aggressive betting language

2. The Audit Engine

Our neural parser scans your messages for tilt-triggers like "one more spin" repeated after a major loss or capitalization spikes that indicate a loss of discipline.

When tilt is detected, TiltCheck assigns a TILT SCORE (0-100). High scores trigger automated profit-locking and system-wide nuke alerts.

3. Casino Trust Scores

Before you deposit at a casino, TiltCheck checks its trust score against our unified database:

  • Domain age — brand new domains get flagged
  • Community reports — verified scam accusations
  • Withdrawal logs — is the casino holding onto funds?

4. Phishing Nuke (LinkCheck)

TiltCheck automatically nukes phishing links posted in Discord. If a URL points to a known drainer or a shadow-casino, we strip it from the chat and blacklist the source instantly.
