What Is TiltCheck?

TiltCheck is a modular, AI-assisted ecosystem built to fix the issues every degen community deals with: scam links, sketchy promos, nerfed bonuses, confusing payouts, trust issues between users, zero transparency from casinos, tilt spirals, and mods burning out trying to manage chaos.

Instead of pretending degens will magically behave or quit gambling, TiltCheck brings transparency, tools, and automation to the space.

This isn't another casino.
This isn't a bankroll scheme.
This is the Degen Transparency Layer.

The Core Philosophy

TiltCheck is built on five principles:

1. Made for Degens. By Degens.™

No judgment. No moralizing. Just tools that actually help.

2. Non-Custodial Always

Users keep their keys, wallets, and money. TiltCheck takes zero custody — ever.

3. Transparency Over Guesswork

Casino trust scores. User trust scores. Fairness signals. Bonus nerf detection. Explainable scoring.

4. Modular, Organic Growth

Each module stands alone and plugs into the ecosystem: JustTheTip, SusLink, CollectClock, FreeSpinScan, Tilt Engine, DA&D, etc.

5. Built for Chaos Reduction

The goal isn't to stop degeneracy — it's to make the chaos smarter, safer, and way more fun.

Legal Rights Detection

(This is the section that makes casinos sweat.)

The Casino Trust Engine doesn't just track fairness — it also checks when a casino may be violating payout timelines, bonus conditions, fraud regulations, AML/KYC requirements, consumer protection laws, provable fairness commitments, and withdrawal rights.

Legal Rights Alert

"Based on your region and this pattern of behavior, you may have grounds for a regulatory complaint or legal action."

This is not legal advice. It's legal awareness — grounded in public regulations.

What Triggers a Legal Rights Alert?

  • Repeated or excessive payout delays
    "Your 48-hour payout took 11 days (x3 times)."
  • Unfair bonus conditions not disclosed upfront
    Nerfs, hidden rollover jumps, silently removed bonuses.
  • Denial of withdrawal without cause
  • Blocked support access
    Casino blocks a user from chat → automatic red flag.
  • Refusal to audit gameplay
    If provable fairness logs mismatch expectations.
  • Violation of regional gambling rules
    (Future: region-aware triggers.)

What TiltCheck Provides

TiltCheck generates a plain-language rights summary, such as:

"This pattern may violate consumer protection laws related to payouts. Recommended next step: request an audit or file a dispute with [Authority]. Save all chats and payout logs."

It also attaches: evidence chain, timestamps, nerf history, payout history, flagged conversations, and support screenshots (if linked).

Users get a complete package to take to: casino arbitration, Kahnawake, Curacao Gaming, MGA, FTC (for US consumers), or a lawyer — depending on region (future automated detection).
