The Guardian Extension

The Chrome Extension is your real-time co-pilot during gameplay sessions. It monitors your activity and provides automated tools to protect you and your bankroll.

Key Extension Features

  • Autovault: An automatic profit-saving engine. When you're on a winning streak on Stake.com, the autovaulter automatically moves a percentage of your profits into your on-site vault, protecting them from being gambled away.
  • Wallet Lock: An emergency cooldown tool. If you feel yourself tilting, you can manually lock a specific amount of your funds for a set duration (e.g., 15 minutes, 1 hour). The funds are inaccessible until the timer expires.
  • Tilt Detector: A sophisticated engine that watches for patterns of tilt—like rage betting and chasing losses—and provides real-time alerts and interventions.
  • License Verifier: Automatically scans the casino you're on and warns you if it's unlicensed or has a bad reputation.
  • "Double Provably Fair" Verifier: A unique system that uses Solana block hashes to verify game fairness, providing a higher level of trust than a casino's internal system.

JustTheTip (Tipping & Airdrops)

A non-custodial system for sending and receiving crypto directly in Discord. All transactions are peer-to-peer on the Solana mainnet.

  • Secure Tipping: Send tips to other users without the risk of a centralized wallet.
  • Airdrop Tools: Easily create and manage airdrops for your community members.
  • Solana Pay: Uses standardized Solana Pay links that you approve in your own wallet, ensuring you are always in control of your funds.
