How It Works

1

We record every bonus. Every day.

Our bots grab the timestamp and value for every bonus drop, building a massive dataset of casino behavior.

2

We do the math to learn their pattern.

Our stats engine analyzes the data to figure out the casino's rhythm—their typical refresh times, bonus amounts, and volatility.

3

We spot when the pattern breaks.

We compare every new bonus against the historical baseline. If something is off—if the amount is nerfed or the timing is weird—we flag it.

4

You get an alert when they get cheap.

If a bonus deviates too far from the norm, we fire off a Discord alert so the whole community knows what's up.
