Q: Does TiltCheck hold my funds?

No. TiltCheck is completely non-custodial. We monitor your activity via browser-side audits and Discord bots, but we never have access to your private keys or your balance. When you hit a limit, we simply freeze your browser's ability to interact with the casino or prompt you to move funds to your own cold wallet.

Q: Is it a gambling site?

Absolutely not. TiltCheck is an audit layer. Think of us as a third-party oversight committee and a digital emergency brake. We provide the math to verify if the casino's RNG is drifting and the tools to help you stay disciplined.

Q: How does the "Emergency Brake" work?

When our algorithms detect "catastrophic tilt behavior" (rapid, increasing bet sizes after losses), the Chrome extension or PWA can forcibly lock the casino's UI until your heart rate cools down. It’s an intervention you set for yourself.

Q: What is a "Rug Score"?

Our Rug Score is a metric derived from real-time analysis of a casino's seed-drifting and withdrawal patterns. We aggregate metadata on-chain to show if a casino is "Stable" or has entered a "Liquidity Crisis" or "Shadow-House" state.
