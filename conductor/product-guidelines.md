# Product Guidelines

This document outlines the design principles, branding, and user experience guidelines for the TiltCheck ecosystem.

## 1. Tone of Voice

The brand's tone is **Degen-Friendly**. This means communication should be:
- **Blunt and Direct:** Get straight to the point. No fluff, no bullshit.
- **Witty & Funny:** Inject humor and wit as often as possible. The persona should be entertaining, not dry.
- **Skeptical:** Acknowledge the harsh realities of the space.
- **Authentic:** Use community slang where appropriate. Cursing is encouraged where it feels authentic and fitting for the audience.

We are an accountability buddy, not a corporate entity. We provide sharp, useful insights without moralizing.

## 2. Branding & Visual Style

- **Dark Theme:** The primary UI for all web applications and components will use a dark theme. Accent colors should be high-contrast and vibrant (e.g., bright greens, magentas, cyans) to draw attention to key data and calls-to-action.
- **No Emojis:** To maintain a clean and professional-but-degen aesthetic, emojis are strictly forbidden in all user interfaces and bot responses. The only exception is for officially sanctioned brand assets or specific, approved use cases.

## 3. User Experience (UX) Principles

- **Actionable Insights:** The primary goal of any data presented to the user is to be actionable. Don't just show raw numbers; provide context, trends, and clear next steps. Every chart should answer a question or prompt a decision.
