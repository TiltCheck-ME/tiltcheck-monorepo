### For Grants:
- Lead with problem statement (gambling safety)
- Emphasize non-custodial approach
- Show community impact potential
- Include technical architecture
- Demonstrate founder commitment

### For Hackathons:

- Working demo > polish
- Video demo crucial
- Emphasize unique angle (degen safety)

### For Female Founder Programs:
- Highlight underrepresented perspective
- Show resilience and resourcefulness
- Connect to broader impact
- Build network proactively

### For Credits:
- Be specific about usage plans
- Show current infrastructure
- Demonstrate growth potential
- Often easier than cash grants
