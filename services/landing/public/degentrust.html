Degen Trust Engine

What is Degen Trust?

The Degen Trust Engine is a non-judgmental reputation system for behavior, good faith, accountability, and scam detection. It provides transparency without punishment, helping users build trust in chaotic environments.

Non-Judgmental Transparency Signals Only

Degen Trust Signals

  • Behavior: Cooldown nudges, accountability, and opt-in safety
  • Scam Detection: Link scanning and evidence artifacts
  • Good Faith: Reputation for fair play and dispute resolution
  • Transparency: Open logs and explainable scores
